Jerry Gaston, BBA


From Raw Data to Actionable Insights...

I've always been driven by the desire to understand the “why” behind things—how processes work, where they can be improved, and how data can drive those improvements. While I initially pursued Computer Science in college with aspirations of becoming a Software Engineer (still pursuing on my own time), I soon discovered my true passion lay in data and its power to tell stories, influence decisions, and solve real-world problems.

After earning my B.B.A. in Data Science and Marketing from Henderson State University, I’ve built a career focused on Human Resources analytics, system integrations, and dashboard development. Today, I serve as a Human Resources Information Systems Analyst at Bank OZK, where I specialize in reporting automation, Power BI dashboard design, and translating HR data into clear, actionable insights for decision-makers. Being at the bank for going on two years, I've assisted with system implementations, data integrations, troubleshooting, audits and maintaining databases. I've written complex queries that feeds various dashboards, reports, HR metrics, and business decisions.

My current work includes building and maintaining dynamic report/dashboards on Talent Acquisition Metrics, Offers Analyis, New Hire Experience, Turnover, and Exit Survey data. I’ve created scalable reports/dashboards using Power BI Report Server, DAX, and Power Query, while optimizing backend processes and creating audits through SQL and ETL logic in Talend. My work empowers HR and business leaders to better understand the employee lifecycle and make informed, data-driven decisions.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between technical and human—transforming raw data into compelling visual stories that spark action. Whether it’s streamlining a manual workflow or identifying patterns in employee engagement, I love being the “problem finder” who brings clarity and impact through analytics. I enjoy the field of data analytics and prepared for where the knowledge I learn takes me.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about community, growth, and creativity. I enjoy gaming, crime shows, music, traveling, and giving back through Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc. I’m always seeking new opportunities that challenge me and allow me to make meaningful contributions through data.

I'm open to new connections, collaborations, and career opportunities that align with my passion for analytics, storytelling, and organizational impact.
