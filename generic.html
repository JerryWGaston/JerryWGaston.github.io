Jerry Gaston, BBA


As I reflect on my journey...

I've always been driven by the desire to understand the "why" behind things - the cause and effect relationships, and the ability to make things better through analysis. Although I didn't fully realize it back then, I've always possessed an analytical mindset.

During my time at Henderson State University, I initially pursued Computer Science, aspiring to become a Software Engineer and create various applications. However, as I progressed into my Sophomore year, I came to the realization that my true passion lies in analysis. It was then that I made a pivotal decision to follow my heart and explore the world of Data Science and Marketing.

Having graduated with a Bachelor of Business Administration, with a Double Major in Data Science & Marketing, I am now eager to embark on a fulfilling career in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence. I have honed my skills in data collection, manipulation, exploratory data analysis, data cleaning & preparation, data mining, data visualization, and effectively communicating my findings.

I am enthusiastic about joining a company that recognizes the value of data-driven insights and is willing to provide me with an opportunity to fuel my passion for Data Analytics. I am confident that my dedication, willingness to learn, and proactive approach will contribute positively to any team.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find joy in playing video games, indulging in crime movies and shows (I've secretly harbored dreams of becoming an FBI agent), giving back to my community through Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Incorporated, and dedicating time to self-improvement, spirituality, and traveling.

I am currently employed at Bank OZK as a Human Resources Information Systems Analyst, where my focus is Reporting. This job has expanded my knowledge and understanding of data.

I am always open to new challenges and experiences, and I firmly believe that the extra step I take to achieve my goals will also translate into exceptional contributions to any organization.
